About Us

Aakash Research Labs is a technical team from SRM-IST which aims at developing both hardware and software. Being a distant lab of IIT-Bombay, it was established for improving and developing efficient ingenious applications across various fields. Aakash Research Labs (ARL) promises to be the torchbearer of cutting-edge technology by developing projects which are improvising different sectors of society and bringing up the technicality.

As an open community, ARL welcomes each and every individual from SRM-IST to make use of this excellent opportunity and experience thinking avant-garde with our team. With a number of projects being proposed, we are looking forward to expand the flow of ideas and do it on a greater scale.
